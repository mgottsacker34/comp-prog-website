Mr. Gottsacker
Computer Programming

Profit projection

This project is designed for groups of two people. There will be one group of three.

Even in his younger years, Larry Page was inventive and enterprising. In college, he reverse-engineered the ink cartridge of an inkjet printer and optimized it for printing large posters.

Problem

Larry offers his services to the University of Michigan's Student Activities Center. Over the course of 5 weeks, his printing company follows a consistent trend:

  • On every day of the week, 1 poster is printed every 7 minutes.
  • On Mondays, an extra poster is printed every 45 minutes.
Larry charges $0.60 for each poster printed. It costs him $0.10 to print each one.

Task

Write a Java program to calculate and display Larry's accumulated profit at the end of each week. Assume he starts with $0.00. Write a document describing the problem, your programmatic approach, and your solution.

Submit your work in the Project_1 folder. Your Java program should be called Project_1.java. The discussion document should be called README.txt.
